description Resumen Ejecutivo

This initiative proposes the phased migration of our tier-1 legacy core banking systems (currently housed in on-premise datacenters) to a secure, compliant AWS cloud environment. The current AS400 infrastructure is approaching end-of-life, leading to increased maintenance costs and limiting our ability to deploy new digital banking products rapidly.

The migration will utilize a "strangler fig" pattern to minimize downtime and risk, running parallel systems during the transition phase. Expected outcomes include a 30% reduction in infrastructure OPEX by Year 3 and a 50% improvement in time-to-market for new API-driven services.
